Privacy Policy

UTeM Confessions Pro Max respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy details how we handle user data across our anonymous confession platform, student utility tools, and advertising partners in compliance with global privacy standards and Google Publisher Policies.

1. Anonymous Confession Submissions

Our confession submission system is engineered to prioritize anonymity. We do not collect personal identifiers, names, phone numbers, email addresses, or IP addresses when you submit a confession. Submissions are transmitted as plaintext statements. To protect your privacy and that of others, please refrain from typing personal names or identifiable details in your confession body.

2. Browser Local Storage & Student Utilities

Our platform includes student utility tools such as the GPA/CGPA Calculator, Exam Countdown Timer, Bus Schedule Viewer, Campus Map, and Academic Calendar. Any grades, target dates, or preferences entered into these widgets are stored and calculated strictly locally within your browser using `localStorage`. This data is never sent to our servers. Local storage is also used to preserve your visual theme preference (Dark/Light mode) and a submission timestamp used solely for anti-spam rate limiting (30-second cooldown).

3. Google AdSense & Advertising Cookies

We use third-party advertising services, including Google AdSense, to serve advertisements when you visit our website. Google uses cookies (such as the DoubleClick DART cookie) to serve ads to users based on their visits to this site and other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of third-party vendor cookies by visiting aboutads.info.

4. Third-Party Ad Networks & Analytics

In addition to Google AdSense, we partner with advertising platforms such as Adsterra and Monetag. These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to serve advertisements and links directly to your browser. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs, and may use cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons to measure the effectiveness of their campaigns and personalize advertising content.

5. Children's Privacy Protection (COPPA)

UTeM Confessions Pro Max is intended for university students and general adult audiences. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a minor has submitted personal information, please contact us immediately so we can remove the content.

6. Data Rights & Contact Information

You have rights regarding your data privacy under applicable privacy frameworks (such as the Personal Data Protection Act - PDPA). If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or content removal, please reach out to our platform administrators via the official community channels.
