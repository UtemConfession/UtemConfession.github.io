UTeM Confession Posting Rules

By submitting a confession or using the UTeM Confession Pro Max channel, you agree to comply with the following community rules. We strive to provide a safe, respectful environment for discussion and anonymous expression.

Rules Breakdown:

  • No Bullying & Harassment: Strictly no targeted attacks, naming and shaming of individuals, spreading malicious gossip, or threat of violence.
  • No Doxxing (Private Info): Do not post student ID card details, phone numbers, email addresses, vehicle plates, or personal photos without explicit written consent.
  • No Illegal Activities: Content promoting drug use, exam cheating/plagiarism rings, or illegal gatherings will be blocked and reported.
  • No Spam or Commercial Ads: Confessions seeking commercial gain, posting affiliate links, or repetitive postings will be filtered out. Use official channels for business.
  • Responsible Discussion: While criticism is allowed, maintain constructive critique regarding academic issues, facilities, or campus administration.

Review Process:

Submissions are queued in a Google Sheet linked to our reviewer bot. Our moderation team screens entries daily. Submission does not guarantee publishing. Approved confessions are posted directly to the official channel.
