BAS.MY Melaka Public Bus Route M10A (To/From UTeM)

Public transit connection connecting UTeM to Melaka Sentral (transit hub). Serves UTeM directly on weekends (Friday to Sunday).

M10A Detailed Schedule & Operations:
  • Weekdays (Monday–Thursday): Runs between Melaka Sentral and MITC (via Batu Berendam) only.
  • Weekends (Friday–Sunday): Service is extended to directly serve Zoo Melaka and UTeM.
  • Operating Hours: 6:00 AM – 8:00 PM daily. Cashless payment only (TNG, Debit Card, QR).

Perpustakaan Laman Hikmah (PLH)

UTeM Laman Hikmah Library offers a massive physical and digital catalog of learning resources, study zones, and modern digital library spaces.

Library Hours

  • Academic Semester Hours: Mon - Thu: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM
    Friday: 8:00 AM - 12:15 PM, 2:45 PM - 10:00 PM
  • Study & Exam Weeks (Weekends): Sat - Sun: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Selected areas)
  • Semester Break Hours: Mon - Fri: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Closed on Weekends)
  • 24-Hour Study Area: Available at Level 2 (Exam weeks)

PLH Gaming Zone & Leisure

  • PlayStation 4 Console & Controller Rental: Rent PS4 controllers and consoles at the Circulation Counter. 3 console sets at Main Campus and 2 sets at Tech Campus. Rate is RM4.00 per hour (includes 2 controllers). Maximum 2 hours per session. Bringing own game CDs is permitted after staff inspection.
  • Free Indoor & Board Games: Borrow dartboards, carrom sets, chess, checkers, and board games for free at the counter.

Booking & Registration:
