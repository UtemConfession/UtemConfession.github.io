Perpustakaan Laman Hikmah (PLH)

UTeM Laman Hikmah Library offers a massive physical and digital catalog of learning resources, study zones, and modern digital library spaces.

Library Hours

  • Academic Semester Hours: Mon - Thu: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM
    Friday: 8:00 AM - 12:15 PM, 2:45 PM - 10:00 PM
  • Study & Exam Weeks (Weekends): Sat - Sun: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Selected areas)
  • Semester Break Hours: Mon - Fri: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Closed on Weekends)
  • 24-Hour Study Area: Available at Level 2 (Exam weeks)

  • PlayStation 4 Rental: Rent PS4 consoles at Circulation Counter (RM4/hr, max 2 hrs). Bring own game CDs permitted.

Scholarship & Financial Aid

Discover government loans, MARA schemes, state foundation grants, corporate awards, and university financial assistance available for UTeM and Malaysian university students.

MARA Sponsorship

MARA TESP & Young Talent Programme (YTP)

✦ Cycle Deadlines: March & August

Convertible loan & sponsorship scheme for Bumiputera students pursuing Diploma, Degree, and Professional studies at IPTA/IPTS in Malaysia including UTeM engineering & IT faculties.

Target: Full-time Diploma / Bachelor Students Official MARA Portal ➔
National Education Loan

PTPTN Higher Education Loan (Pinjaman PTPTN)

✦ Deadlines: April 30 & Sept 30

Primary tertiary education loan scheme for Malaysian undergraduates. First-Class Honours graduates from UTeM may qualify for complete loan-to-scholarship conversion (bebas bayaran balik).

Target: All Malaysian Undergraduates MyPTPTN Application Portal ➔
State Foundation

Biasiswa & Pinjaman Yayasan Negeri (Yayasan Melaka / YPM)

✦ Deadlines: Open Quarterly

Financial aid & tertiary education grants provided by state foundations (Yayasan Melaka, Yayasan Pelajaran MARA, Yayasan Johor, Yayasan Selangor, etc.) for eligible state-born or resident students.

Target: Melaka Born / Resident Students Yayasan Melaka Portal ➔
Foundation Grant

Yayasan Tunku Abdul Rahman (YTAR) & Yayasan Bank Rakyat (YBR)

✦ Deadline: April - August Annually

Prestigious undergraduate scholarships and Pembiayaan Pendidikan Boleh Ubah (PPBU) offering full tuition, monthly allowances, and leadership development programs for high-achieving undergraduates.

Target: High-Achieving Undergraduates
YTAR Site ➔ YBR Site ➔
Federal Government

JPA MyScholarship (Program Penajaan JPA PIDN/LSPN)

✦ Deadline: May - June Annually

Jabatan Perkhidmatan Awam (JPA) undergraduate sponsorship for Malaysian students pursuing Degree programs in engineering, computer science, technology, and applied sciences.

Target: Engineering & Tech Degree Students JPA MyScholarship Portal ➔
UTeM Campus Aid

UTeM Student Welfare Fund (Tabung Kebajikan Pelajar UTeM)

✦ Status: Open Year-Round

Emergency financial aid, zakat assistance, food vouchers, and medical assistance managed directly by Pejabat Hal Ehwal Pelajar & Alumni (HEPA UTeM) for active students facing financial hardship.

Target: Active UTeM Students in Need UTeM HEPA Welfare Portal ➔
