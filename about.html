About UTeM Confessions

UTeM Confessions is an unofficial student platform created to provide a space for students to share anonymous confessions and gain other helpful infos around the campus.

This platform is independently managed by student developers and is not an official website of Universiti Teknikal Malaysia Melaka (UTeM).
